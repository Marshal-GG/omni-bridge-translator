# Privacy Policy - Omni Bridge

**Last Updated: March 2026**

Your privacy is a priority at Omni Bridge. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our live translation platform.

## 1. Information We Collect
Omni Bridge is designed to minimize data collection. 

### Audio Data
- **Capture**: The app captures live audio from your selected input (Microphone or System Audio).
- **Processing**: Audio is processed locally and streamed to third-party AI providers (Google, NVIDIA, OpenAI) for real-time transcription and translation.
- **Persistence**: Audio data is NOT stored by Omni Bridge on any server. It is processed in-memory and discarded after translation.

### Local Data (Caption History)
- If you use the "Caption History" feature, transcriptions are saved **locally on your device**.
- This data never leaves your machine unless you explicitly use a synchronization feature.

## 2. Third-Party Data Sharing
To provide its core functionality, Omni Bridge sends text or audio segments to:
- **Google Cloud/Translate**
- **NVIDIA Riva / NIM**
- **OpenAI (Whisper)**

These providers have their own privacy policies. Omni Bridge does not control how these third parties handle data once it enters their infrastructure, but we only share the minimum data necessary for translation.

## 3. Data Security
We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (WebSocket/HTTPS) between your device and AI providers.

## 4. User Rights & Control
- **History Management**: You can clear your searchable caption history at any time within the app settings.
- **Microphone Access**: You can revoke microphone permissions through your operating system settings.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or service. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

## 6. Contact
For privacy-related questions, please contact the developer via the official repository or the support email listed in the **About** section of the app.
